About Me

Hello! My name is April Tsai, a Political Science and Economics second-year student at the University of Michigan with a strong interest in consulting, public service, human resources, and UX design.

I am passionate about social impact and bettering the lives of people around me through technology and design. As an avid public speaker, adaptable leader, and astute team player, I strive to bring creativity, enthusiasm, reliability, and a global perspective into anything I do.

Fun Fact: My name is April, but I was not born in April!
